Our Beginning

From a Family Kitchen to Nagpur's Finest

Zafran was founded on a single cherished belief: that the grandeur of Mughal cuisine deserves to be experienced in every corner of India. What began as generations-old family recipes — passed from grandmother to mother, from kitchen whispers to feast-day aromas — has grown into Nagpur's most beloved premium dining destination.

Named after Zafran — the Persian word for saffron — our restaurant is a tribute to the golden spice that has perfumed Mughal kitchens for centuries. Every dish we serve carries the weight of history and the warmth of home.

Our Commitment

100% Halaal, Always Fresh

We take our Halaal commitment with absolute seriousness. Every piece of chicken and mutton is sourced daily from certified Halaal butchers who share our values of ethical, humane, and religiously compliant meat preparation.

There are no frozen shortcuts at Zafran. Our spice blends are hand-ground each morning. Our gravies are slow-simmered for hours. Our biryanis are sealed and cooked in the dum tradition — patience and craft at every step.

"We will never compromise on the quality of our ingredients. Our guests trust us with their dining experience, and that trust is sacred to us."

— Chef Founder, Zafran

Our Kitchen Standards

Behind every dish at Zafran is a kitchen that operates like a well-oiled, spotlessly clean machine. Our chefs wear food-grade gloves and masks. Surfaces are sanitised between every preparation. Refrigerators are calibrated to precise temperatures. Pest control is conducted weekly.

  • ✓ FSSAI certified kitchen with active licence
