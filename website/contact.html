Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you serve Halaal meat only?

Yes, 100%. All chicken and mutton served at Zafran is sourced exclusively from certified Halaal butchers. This is a non-negotiable commitment at our restaurant.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! We deliver through Swiggy and Zomato. You can also call us directly for orders above ₹500 within 5km radius.

Can we host private events?

Absolutely! We offer private dining arrangements for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate dinners and family gatherings. Contact us to discuss custom menus and table setups.

Is parking available?

Yes, parking is available in front of and near the restaurant. Our location near ICICI Bank also has ample street parking on Jafar Nagar Road.

Do you have veg options?

Yes! We have a rich selection of vegetarian dishes — from Shahi Paneer and Dal Makhani to Veg Dum Biryani and Shammi Kebabs. Veg and non-veg are prepared in separate areas.

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all major debit/credit cards, and net banking. No minimum card amount.
